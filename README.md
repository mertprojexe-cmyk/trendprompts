## 1. Overview

TrendPrompts is a prompt library application.  
The App allows users to browse prompt content, view example images, and copy prompt text to the device clipboard.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### We do not collect personal data directly

We do **not** require account creation and we do **not** directly collect personal information such as name, phone number, or address.

### Technical and network data

When the App loads online content (for example, images hosted on third-party servers), your device may send standard network requests (such as IP address and device/network metadata) to those servers.  
This processing is performed by your device/operating system and relevant service providers.

---

## 3. Device Permissions and Access

The App may use:

- **Internet access**: to load app content and remote media assets.
- **Clipboard write access**: only when you explicitly press the copy button to copy prompt text.

The App does **not** read clipboard content in the background.

---

## 4. How Data Is Used

Data (including technical network data) is used only to:

- deliver and display app content,
- enable prompt copy functionality,
- maintain app reliability and performance.

We do not sell personal data.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party infrastructure (such as content hosting/CDN providers and platform services) to deliver media and app functionality.  
Those third parties may process technical data according to their own privacy policies.

## 6. Data Storage and Retention

TrendPrompts stores app content locally on the device as needed for functionality.  
No user account database is maintained by us for this App.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored app data is removed according to your device settings.
